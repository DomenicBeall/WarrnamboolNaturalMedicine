About the Clinic

We take pride in helping the body to heal itself...



We pride ourselves on providing intelligent, holistic and effective treatment strategies to help solve your health dilemmas. We use the oldest forms of medicine known to man (Acupuncture and Herbal Medicine) which have been around for thousands of years, as well as an exciting, new approach for treating musculoskeletal problems (Bowen therapy). This clinic was established in 1991 and we are proud to have contributed to the health and wellbeing of the people of Warrnambool for the past three decades.

Kate and Graham Pennington are both qualified practitioners and registered members of AHPRA. Accordingly, private health fund rebates are available for consultations at the clinic.

We are passionate complementary health care providers. This means we help the body to heal itself, using holistic approaches. This is reflected in our assessment and treatment techniques. We assist the body to realign and repair, working from a wider, more global perspective. Each person is assessed holistically and then provided with a treatment based on their unique presentation.
